## 3. Voice & tone rules

The most important lesson from drafting so far:

> **Surface = crafted. Depth = honest.**

Anything personal or vulnerable belongs *below the fold of a click* — a reward for the visitor who came closer. The homepage is a handshake; the deeper layers are the conversation after. Don't broadcast interior life on the front door.

- Confident, not precious
- Sharp copy over soulful copy on the surface
- Show, don't tell — let the work carry weight
- Specific beats poetic when a stakeholder is skimming

---

## 4. Visual direction

**Reference frame: Hero Studios (herostudios.tv) — as direction, NOT a clone.** A specific reference website is being supplied separately to build from directly.

We borrow the *energy*, not the layout or code:

- Bold, confident, agency-grade
- Light / white (or warm off-white) background
- Big display sans-serif typography
- Work-forward — visuals first, minimal copy
- Tile grids where the content supports them
- Fast and modern, not slow and reverent

**Note on history:** an earlier direction (dreamy / cinematic / nature-inspired / dark, "Our Universe"-style) was explored and set aside. It is **not** the current direction — the road not taken.
