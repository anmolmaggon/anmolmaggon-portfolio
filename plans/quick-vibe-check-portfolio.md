## Problem

Job seekers came to company review pages with urgent questions about culture, leadership, and balance, but the product made them do the work manually. They had to scroll through a chronological wall of long reviews, mentally group patterns, and decide for themselves what the consensus was.

## Approach

We moved the page from raw feed to insight engine. AI-generated sentiment summaries led the page, raw reviews became proof instead of the starting point, and the review cards were rebuilt for scanning speed instead of social-feed behavior.

## 3 Decisions

1. **Show the receipts** - every AI insight needed a path back to the exact source reviews, so trust came from evidence rather than model confidence.
2. **Insights first, details later** - the page answered the user's broad culture question before asking them to read individual reviews.
3. **Scannability over social behavior** - review cards were simplified so the feed supported quick evaluation, not comments, dislikes, or social-thread complexity.
